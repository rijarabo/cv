About Me

I’m a Master’s of Urban Planning Candidate at NYU Wagner specializing in Environment, Infrastructure, and Transportation.

I have a Bachelor of Arts degree in Economics and over 6 years of working experience in economic research, public policy design and implementation.

More recently I became a data storyteller. I like to engage audiences through multiple types of visualizations: presentations, maps, visual representations and data dashboards.

As an economist I am used to question the different market forces in the development of cities. As an urban planner I am constantly gaining awareness of the built environment and the critical elements required for successful place making.
